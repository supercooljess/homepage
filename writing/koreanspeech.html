"The Reason Why I Like Gonggal Bread"


Translation:

In my hometown, there is a bakery that I often used to go to with my grandmother.  There, there was a huge bread known as gonggal bread.  'It's bigger than all the other bread, but why is it only two dollars?' I thought.  It was strange.  My grandmother bought me one, and as I excitedly took a bite, 'Huh?  What is this?'  The bread was hollow.  At first I expected it to be hard and tasteless, but the more I ate, it became rich and delicious.  It was different from what I had originally thought.

When I was in middle school, I did wrestling, and I was the smallest on my team.  When I competed, everyone thought I would lose, and I would always get flipped my my opponents.  But I trained diligently every day, and eventually, I won.  From that moment on, big opponents didn't scare me, and whenever I competed, I thought of gonggal bread.

When I came to high school, I decided to study mathematics, but the math problems became extremely difficult.  Math is my favorite subject, but sometimes after several hours I can't even solve one problem.  When that happens, I think of gonggal bread.  Though a question looks long and complicated, if you approach it with confidence, you can find a simple answer.

These days, I have many tests, interviews, and other difficult work.  Sometimes before I begin, I am intimidated and want to give up.  But if you prepare well and don't give up, success will surely come.  Everyone, when you face hard times, I encourage you to think of gonggal bread.
